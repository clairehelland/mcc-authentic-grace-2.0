I LOVE Authentic Grace. I’ve had some of their pieces for years and they hold up nicely. And make no mistake, I’ve really put some of these clothes to the test with three small kids! I’ve never had a juice spill that didn’t lift out easily, which is just the kind of clothing a busy SAHM like me needs in my life. Did I mention everything I own from this store can go in a washing machine? P.S. I never feel “frumpy” in Authentic Grace, only beautiful.

My first purchase from Authentic Grace was gorgeous, but after a couple weeks, I decided it wasn’t for me. I always feel confident in my Authentic Grace gear! You can’t go wrong with multi-functional fashion, and it's so easy to order and ship quickly. Authentic Grace is my new favorite go-to site for clothes shopping. And I get a ton of compliments from my co-workers!
